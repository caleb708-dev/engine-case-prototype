## What's Inside

### MVP — V1 (Ships in Weeks 1–6)
Targets the three highest-pain touchpoints in the group organizer's journey:
- **AI Contract Copilot** — explains every hotel contract clause in plain language, flags risk, suggests counter-terms
- **Guest Booking Dashboard** — real-time view of who's booked, attrition countdown, one-tap reminders
- **Self-Serve Modification Portal** — handles headcount changes and room swaps without calling an agent

### Moonshot — Full Vision
The full-stack group travel OS, built around the 11-star experience framework:
- **AI Prediction Engine** — surfaces and locks the perfect trip before the organizer even announces dates
- **AI Contract Negotiator** — pushes counter-proposals directly to hotel partners; learns from every accepted/rejected term
- **Live Trip Command Center** — real-time booking heat map with an AI nudge engine that runs a behavioral sequence on unbooked guests
- **Make It Unforgettable** — curated group dining, activities, and transport; one-tap booking; Engine earns 10–15% commission

## Context

This prototype accompanies a Staff PM case study addressing three questions:
1. Map the full groups journey — where does it break down and what would you build?
2. How would you prioritize improvements? Where does AI reduce manual effort?
3. What does success look like and how would you measure it?

**North star metric:** Room nights booked per month
